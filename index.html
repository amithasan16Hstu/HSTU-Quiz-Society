About Our Society

Expanding Horizons Through Quizzing

The HSTU Quiz Society is a premier student organization dedicated to fostering intellectual growth and promoting quizzing culture at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University.

Founded with the vision to "Know the Universe Through Knowledge," we organize regular quizzes, workshops, and competitions to challenge minds and expand horizons.
