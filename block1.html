About US

The HSTU Quiz Society (HQS) is a student-led organization at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University (HSTU) that promotes a passion for knowledge and quizzing. It organizes intra-university and inter-university quiz contests, helping students enhance their critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Regular Events

We organize various events throughout the year to engage students in intellectual activities and promote knowledge sharing across different disciplines.

Online Quiz

Participate in our digital quiz competitions from anywhere. Test your knowledge against peers in real-time with our interactive online platform.

Offline Quiz

Experience the thrill of live quizzing with buzzer rounds, visual challenges, and face-to-face competition in our auditorium events.

Public Speaking

Develop your oratory skills through our workshops and speaking competitions. Gain confidence in presenting ideas clearly and effectively.

Leadership Growth

Take on organizational roles in our events to develop leadership qualities, team management skills, and event planning expertise.

Signature and Recruitment Events

Signature Events

Inter Faculty Quiz Competition

Bridging Minds, Igniting Competition.

Our flagship annual event where faculties compete for intellectual supremacy. Features multiple rounds testing diverse knowledge areas with prestigious trophies at stake.

More Details

Event structure:

  • Preliminary written elimination round
  • Quarter-finals: Audio-visual rounds
  • Semi-finals: Rapid fire buzzer round
  • Grand finale: Mixed format challenge

Emerging Quiz Competition

Rise to the Challenge, Unleash Your Potential.

Designed specifically for first and second year students to showcase their quizzing talent. Focuses on fundamental knowledge with mentorship from senior quizzers.

More Details

Event highlights:

  • Exclusive to junior students
  • Training sessions before competition
  • Certificates for all participants
  • Spotlight on emerging talent

Special Day Quiz Competition

Celebrate Knowledge, Honor the Moment.

Themed quizzes organized on national and international important days to commemorate historical events and raise awareness about significant issues.

More Details

Regular special day quizzes:

  • Independence Day Quiz (March)
  • Victory Day Quiz (December)
  • International Mother Language Day Quiz (February)
  • Science Day Quiz (November)

Recruitment Events

Quiz Master

Empowering Minds, Inspiring Excellence

Annual recruitment drive to select and train new quiz masters who will host our events. Includes rigorous testing of question framing and presentation skills.

More Details

Selection process:

  • Written test on general knowledge
  • Question framing assignment
  • Mock quiz hosting evaluation
  • Final interview with senior quiz masters

Quiz Leader

Lead the Challenge, Inspire the Future.

Leadership positions for organizing and coordinating quiz events. Develop organizational skills while managing teams and event logistics.

More Details

Responsibilities include:

  • Event planning and coordination
  • Team management
  • Sponsorship acquisition
  • Public relations

Member Recruitment

Join the Quest, Shape the Future.

Biannual membership drive welcoming new students to the society. Open to all HSTU students passionate about quizzing and knowledge sharing.

More Details

Member benefits:

  • Priority participation in events
  • Training workshops
  • Networking opportunities
  • Leadership development
